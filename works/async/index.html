Async

2017

Short film

Created for the International Short Film Competition initiated by Ryuichi Sakamoto and Apichatpong Weerasethakul.

Built from documentary footage recorded across different places and periods, the film assembles fragments of urban life, industry, landscape and human movement into a meditation on time, impermanence and isolation.

Async originated from an invitation to respond cinematically to music from async. Working with an archive of documentary footage gathered across different places and periods, the film assembles fragments of infrastructure, urban environments, industry, landscapes and human presence into a reflection on time, transience and the unstable relationship between individual life and larger systems. The work draws inspiration from cinematic approaches associated with observational and non-narrative filmmaking, where rhythm, accumulation and juxtaposition become primary narrative forces.

Created using: Ryuichi Sakamoto — Life, Life — Andata

The work was later shared on Ryuichi Sakamoto's official Facebook page.
